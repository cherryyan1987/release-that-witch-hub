## Why Release That Witch stands out

Release That Witch is not just a fantasy title with witches and magic.

Its appeal comes from how it combines:

- engineering logic
- kingdom-building
- political conflict
- production and reform
- character relationships
- long-term strategic progression

That mix gives the story a different rhythm from more conventional fantasy or action-first series.

---

## Anime vs manga

One of the most common questions from new readers is why the anime feels different from the manga.

A practical way to describe it is:

- the **anime** feels more screen-structured, staged, and adaptation-driven
- the **manga** feels more direct, fast to enter, and easier to consume visually

This project exists partly to make those differences easier to understand.
